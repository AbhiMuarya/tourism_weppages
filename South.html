South India

Location: Below the Vindhya Range

Major Cities: Chennai, Hyderabad, Bangalore, Cochin, Pondicherry

Holy Destinations: Rameshwaram, Sabarimala, Thiruvananthapuram

Holiday Destinations: Alappuzha Backwaters, Southern Beaches, Lakshadweep

South India is linguistic and geographically cultural region of India. South India captures the Vindhyanchal mountain ranges, Satpura and Narmada River. South India is linguistic and geographic culture land of India. Where the blend of rich heritage and nature can be witnessed. The Muslim sway was fleeting at the peninsular tip of India where as peripheral influence on Hinduism in South was twisted by sliest quantity. The Temple towns of South are quite unlike those of the North and superbly Vibrant.

South India basically covers the four extoic states of India: Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala and Tamil Nadu and the Union Territory of Pondicherry. The South India tourist destinations are tourism hot spots offering a right blend of wildlife, beaches, temples, history, landscapes that allure millions of tourists every year.

The backwaters of Kerala, pristine beaches of Lakshadweep, temples of Tamil Nadu, the wildlife belt of the Mudumalai and Bandipur forests, the world heritage sites of Karnataka is what makes the visit to South India a perfect vacation. Travel to South India with Meruka Tours to have a taste of this part of India and get charmed by the unique culture and tradition of South India.
